Business Basics

What is a business?
A business is something that solves a problem and gets paid for solving it.

Where do ideas come from?
Ideas come from noticing problems: things that are slow, annoying, expensive, or missing.

How do you make money with no money?
You trade your time and skills instead of cash — like editing, designing, tutoring, or managing communities.

Common beginner mistakes:

  • Trying to be perfect before starting
  • Copying others without understanding the problem they solve
  • Quitting after the first failure
