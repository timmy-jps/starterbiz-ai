Learn business without being a “business person”

This site is made for beginners and teens. No fancy words, no boring textbooks. Just clear explanations about how business works and how people actually make money.

Business basics

What is a business?

A business is just a system that:
1) Solves a problem for someone
2) Gets paid for solving it

How do people get ideas?

Most ideas come from noticing problems:
• Something is annoying or slow
• Something is too expensive
• Something doesn’t exist yet but should

How do you make money with no money?

You trade your time and skills instead of cash. Examples: designing, editing, writing, tutoring, managing communities.

Common beginner mistakes

  • Trying to be “perfect” before starting
  • Copying others without understanding the problem they solve
  • Quitting after the first failure

The goal isn’t to build a huge company at 14. The goal is to learn how business works while you’re young.
